Reading Snapshot • The 5-Level Diagnostic

Welcome to the Reading Snapshot.

Let’s look under the hood and find your child’s exact reading bottleneck. Your child will read both real words and Alien words. We use Alien words because they force the brain to prove it knows the phonics code — rather than relying on memory.

How It Works

1

Sit across from your child. Show them the screen so they can read each word aloud.

2

Words appear one at a time. After your child reads it, tap the word card to rate how they did.

3

If they stall for more than 3 seconds, tap the word, mark it Guessed / Skipped, and say “Let’s try the next one.”

4

A 1-minute timer runs in the background. It is only for your reference — you can hide it so it does not stress your child.

5

Assessment is NOT teaching time. Do not correct or hint. Just observe and rate each word honestly.

6

When time is up, the app will guide you through a short celebration before revealing your child’s reading profile.
